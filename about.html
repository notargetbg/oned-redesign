    ON-ED Idea

    ON-ED is a global educational platform that empowers users from all around the world to teach and learn online. We are developing a resource that helps instructors to build online courses and share their expertise, and students to take courses across various categories and fields.

    ON-ED Mission

    to encourage personal, material and professional development of our members!

    ON-ED Goal

    to create the first global educational marketplace, connecting teachers and learners from all around the world in a single social educational environment.

    ON-ED Vision

    to combine all possible types of online learning: video / audio courses, webinars, individual training, corporate training, as well as distance higher education.
