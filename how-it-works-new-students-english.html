    What do we offer?

    We know what prevents busy people to attend as many courses as they like. That is why we offer you an opportunity to learn and develop your skills through several types of courses.

    Live courses

    These courses are a combination of video courses and webinars. They give you an opportunity to study for a long periods of time. Not only will you be watching video lectures, but you’ll also have a regular practical sessions with your teacher in the virtual room.

    Webinars

    Online seminars in real time offer the same experience as in a real attendance, except that:

    you choose a place

    you save time and money

    you get a recording of the event

    You can hear and see the lecturer, ask questions (through your microphone or chat), watch an interactive presentation or see what the lecturer is writing on the interactive board.
    You need only a computer, a good internet connection, working microphone and headphones (if you participate in a discussion). Try out our virtual room.

    Pre-made courses (Video courses)

    Watch or listen video/audio lectures, read useful materials and presentations whenever you want. All you need to participate in pre-made courses is a computer or a mobile device, with a good internet connection.

    How can I enroll in a course?

    If you have chosen a paid course, you can make a payment in one of the following ways:

    -Using Epay or EasyPay payment methods;
    -Using PayPal;
    -By bank transfers;
    -Using a credit card;

    Once you’ve chosen the right course for you, you can become a participant in it by pressing the Enroll button. In the curriculum of the course you will find video/audio materials and presentations. Additional materials can be found in the library, which is located below the curriculum. You can get more information about the lecturer from his profile, which is located on the right side of the screen.

    If you’ve chosen to enroll in a webinar, press Enroll to join the other participants. Return to the course on the announced date and time and enter the webinar room by pressing the button located under the curriculum.
    For more instructions click here.

    What happens after I enroll in a course?

    You have unlimited online access to the course (while it’s on the website).

    After course completion, you will receive a certificate, if the teacher provides one. You can find information, whether you get a certificate or not in the description of the course.

    We recommend that you rate the course and the teacher and leave a comment. This can be useful to the other participants.

    If you are not satisfied with the course and want your money back, you are entitled to make a claim within 7 days, accompanied by a written explanation.

    Downloading and recording any materials from the course is prohibited, as well as giving access to the site to other people. If a violation is established, the access to the course and the site is terminated.

    If case of a failed course, fraud, deceptive behavior of the teacher or the others, please contact us.
