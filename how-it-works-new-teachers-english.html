    What do we offer?

    You will not only be able to use all the right tools for online training, but you will also have the opportunity to choose the type of course you want to teach:

    Live courses

    These courses are a combination of video courses and webinars. They allow you to prepare a long-term training plan, that can include both video/audio lectures and live meetings in the virtual room. That way the lessons that may need to be repeated can be recorded, and you can put all your efforts into practical class sessions.

    Webinars

    By creating an online seminar, you can broadcast your courses in real time, and lead a discussion with the participants while presenting different materials. Everything happens in a virtual classroom equipped with all the necessary tools (virtual board, interactive presentation, chat, video/audio, etc.).

    Pre-made courses (Video courses)

    Record your video or audio lectures, add presentations and any other useful materials, structure your curriculum and create an attractive video course. A one-time effort that will provide you with a passive income stream. We offer a professional recording for your video lectures. For more information about the service contact us, we’re always available.

    What do you need to do?

    Once you’ve registered you can proceed with creating your profile. Tell us more about yourself and your professional experience. That helps users make their decision which course to take. Your course will be rated using our evaluation system.

    Fill your data correctly. Anonymous profiles, nicknames or fake names are not acceptable and would not seem credible to the users.

    Make your course attractive. Choose suitable cover or demo video, write an interesting title, and create an accurate, informative description. The more materials your course includes, the more participants you’ll attract. If you have any questions, or you need help, contacts us, we’re always available.

    If you provide a certificate of course completion, place this information in a prominent place in your course, preferably in a large print. It will greatly increase the chance of sales.

    Once you’ve created your course, it must be checked before publishing. It should be completed correctly and it should not include obscene or inappropriate materials.

    What happens after I enroll in a course?

    Registering and creating your course is free. For every paid course that took place we deduct 20% + transaction tax (1-3%) commission for each enrolled student.

    You’ll receive:

    • Consultation to help you create a course;
    • All the tools the platform offers, no matter the type of course or training;
    • Technical and customer support;
    • Payment systems;
    • Publication of your course in our catalogue;
    • Participation in joint marketing and promotional campaigns;

    Additional services not included in this plan are:

    • Individual promotion and advertising of the course;
    • Video recording and editing;
    • Translation to other languages;
    • Participation in ‘recommended courses’;
    • If you want to use additional services, please contact us ;

    Free courses or webinars can be held free on our platform.

    You get you profit at the end of each month (if a purchase is made in the last days of the month, you will get your percentage at the end of the next month), and the money is transferred to your bank account. If a teacher is registered as a legal entity (company), the money is transferred against an invoice.

    ON-ED gives each client the opportunity to waive participation in the course, after a written request within 7 days after purchase.
