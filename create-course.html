    Translation Program

    ON-ED Translation Program is designed to erase language barriers and help your course reach much larger audience in different countries around the world. Our teams of translators will translate fully your course on different languages, including voice-over or subtitles, sales copy, name and localization. Currently we support translations to and from English, Russian and Bulgarian with more to come.

    Translators translate courses participating in the program by their own discretion so there is no guarantee that your course will be translated. Moreover we admit only top-quality courses. The translators receive shared IP on that language version, revenue share, and exclusivity on that market. The course is fully localized and separate from the original version.
